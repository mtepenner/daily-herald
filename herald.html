By Sentinel News Service

Court Weighs Farmworker Wage Cuts

A federal court in Fresno heard arguments regarding a new rule that could cut minimum wages for H-2A immigrant farmworkers to approximately $13.70 an hour. Labor advocates warn this could drive down pay for domestic agricultural workers nationwide.
